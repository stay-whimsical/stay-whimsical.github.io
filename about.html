About

Stay Whimsical is Karen, Sara, and Pablo. We're hackers who love magic and stories and were given the advice to "stay whimsical" by a marker-bearing saint who visited our major first project, a barely-functioning Screamchess.

We all work full-time as professionals in software of different stripes, currently situation in the greater New York area.
